## Reanimation Protocols
If your army faction is NECRONS, during your command phase, all units with this ability activate its reanimation protocols. When a unit activates reanimation protocols it reanimates d3 wounds. Each time a unit reanimates a wound: 
- If that unit containes one or more models with fewer than their starting number of wounds remaining, select one of those models; that model regains one lost wound
- If all models in that unit have their startin number of wounds but the unit is not at its starting strength, one destroyed model is returned to that unit with 1 wound remaining. 


## Army Protocols
Each turn, your warlord may invoke one of the following protocols by spending 1 AC at the start of the turn. Each protocol may only be invoked once per game. C'TAN SHARD units may never be affected by a Command Protocol. 
### Protocol of the Eternal Guardian
When this protocol is invoked, whenever an ranged attack targets a unit within 9" of a CHARACTER unit from your army. If that unit has not moved they receive a 6+ Cover save against that attack. 
### Protocol of the Sudden Storm
When this protocol is invoked, add 1 to the move characteristic of friendly NECRON units with 9" of a CHARACTER unit.
### Protocol of the Vengeful Stars
When this protocol is invoked, if Necron unit within 9" of a CHARACTER unit from your makes a ranged attack, if that attack is within Short range it has the Ignores Cover ability. 
### Protocol of the Hungry Void
When this protocol is invoked, add 1 to the Strength of Melee weapons equipped to Necron units that made a charge move and are within 9" of a CHARACTER unit.
### Protocol of the Undying Legions
When this protocol is invoked, whenever a NECRON unit within 9" of a CHARACTER unit invokes its reanimation protocols, add 1 to the result.
### Protocol of the Conquering Tyrant
Add 3" to the range of NECRON CHARACTER aura abilities and Command Protocol Abilities.

## Command Protocol Abilities
Some NECRON CHARACTER models already have a Command Protocol Ability, tagged with the words 'Command Protocol'. In the activation phase, the character may spend 1 AC to activate this ability once per turn. When you do so, select one friendly non CHARACTER NECRON unit within 9" of that model. Until the end of the the turn, that unit is affected by this Command Protocol Ability. C'TAN SHARD units may never be affected by a Command Protocol Ability. Each character may only activate its Command Protocol Ability once per turn, and a unit can only be affected by one Command Protocol Ability at a time. The most recent Command Protocol Ability takes precedence. 

## Abilities
### Tesla X
Weapons with this ability have the Sustained Fire X ability. However, you this weapon may not become jammed.(Meaning if you roll a 1 you can still fire this 1 next turn).

### Necrodermis
Models with this ability have a 4+ invulnerable save. In addition, when an attack is allocated to a model with this ability, subtract 1 from the damage characteristic of that attack to a minimum of 1. 

### Quantum Shielding
Models with this ability have a 5+ invulnerable save. In addtion, when a penetration test made against models with this ability, an umodified result(The roll of the d6 excluding the strength modifier) of 1-3 always fails. 

## Warlord Traits
### Enduring Will
Each time an attack is allocated to this Warlod, subtract 1 from the Damage characteristic of that attack(to a minimum of 1).

### Eternal Madness
Add 1 to this models WS. When this model fights, you may instead add D3 to this models WS, but it suffers 1 mortal wound. 

### Immortal Pride
This model may never perform a fall back move but it gains the following ability:
Immortal Pride(Aura): While a friendly NECRON unit is within 6" of this model(Excluding C'TAN SHARD units), each time a battleshock test is taken for that unit, ignore any or all modifiers. 

### Thrall of the Silent King
Add 3" to the range of this WARLORD's aura abilities. In addition, whenever this WARLORD uses a [Command Protocol Ability](#command-protocol-abilities) add 3" to the range of that ability. 

### Implacable Conqueror(Aura)
While a friendly NECRON(Excluding C'TAN SHARD units), is within 6" of this WARLORD, you may reroll charge rolls made for that unit. 

### Honourable Combatant
When this WARLORD is selected to fight, it may engage in honourable combat. If it does select one enemy CHARACTER unit within engagement range. Until the end of the phase, add 2 to the Attacks Characteristic of melee weapons equipped by this warlord when targeting that CHARACTER unit.

## Army Strategems

### Resurrection Protocols 1CP
When: Any Phase \
Target: One NECRON INFANTRY CHARACTER who was just destroyed. You can use this strategem on that model even though it was just destroyed. \
Effect: Before the next unit activates, set your model back up on the battlefield as close as possible to where it was destroyed outside of engagement range, with half of its starting number of wounds remaining. \
Restrictions: Each model may only be targeted with this strategem once per battle

### Reactive Reanimation 1CP
When: Any Phase, just after an enemy unit shoots or fights \
Target: One NECRON unit from your army that had one of its models destroyed as the result of the attacking units attacks \
Effect: Your unit activates its reanimation protocols, adding 1 to the result if it is under the effect of a [Command Protocol Ability](#command-protocol-abilities) . 

### Techno-Orachular Targeting 1CP
When: Activation Phase, just before a penetration test is made for a ranged attack by a NECRONS unit from your army \
Target: That NECRON unit \
Effect: Do not make a penetration test, the result is considered to be an automatic 6. \
Restrictions: You cannot roll on the critical damage table unless you do internal damage. 

### Quantum Deflection
When: Activation Phase, just after an enemy unit has declared its attacks(ranged or melee) \
Target: NECRON VEHICLE with the Quantum Shielding ability that was selected as the target of one or more attacks \
Effect: Until the end of the phase, models in the unit have a 4+ Invulnerable save instead of a 5+.
