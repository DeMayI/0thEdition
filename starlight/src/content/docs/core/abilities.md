---
title: Abilities
description: An overview of all the generic abilities
---

### Heavy X
If the unit has remained stationary, improve the characteristic denoted by X by 1.

### Sustained Fire X
You may Roll X D6. For each dice, consult the table below
| Roll | Result |
| ---- | ---- |
| 1 | Add 1 to the attack characteristic of the weapon but it becomes jammed and cannot be fired next turn |
| 2-3 | Add 1 to the attack characteristic of the weapon |
| 4-5 | Add 2 to the attack characteristic of the weapon |
| 6 | Add 3 to the attack characteristic of the weapon |

### Assault
This weapon does not suffer a hit roll penalty for running. 

### Cleave X
You can select models within X" of this model as a valid target of melee attacks made with this melee weapon. 

### Pistol
Weapons with this ability may be fired in engagement range so long as they target the a unit within engagement range of this model. Weapons with this ability cannot be fired at the same time as non pistol weapons unless you have the MONSTER/VEHICLE keyword. 

### Melta X
While a weapon with this ability is fired within short range, increase the strength of the weapon by X. 

### Armorbane
When a weapon with is ability makes a penetration test, you may reroll that test. 

### Precision
Weapons with the precision ability can ignore Look Out Sir. 

### Breaching X
When any wound roll is made for a weapon with this ability, if the umodified result is greater than or equal to X, improve the AP of that attack by 2.

### Poison X
When any wound roll is made for a weapon with this ability, if the umodifed result is less than or equal to X you may reroll the dice.

### Rending X
When a hit roll is made for a weapon with this ability,  an umodified result of X+ is considered a critical hit. Do not make a wound roll for these hits, and consider the wound roll to be an automatic 6. 

### Stealth
When a unit with this ability is selected as the target of a ranged attack, subtract 1 from that attack's hit roll. This unit can only be selected as a valid target if it is within 18" of the attacking unit

### Infiltrate
Units with this ability can be set up anywhere on the board not within the enemy's deployment zone more than 9 inches away from the enemy's deployment zone and any enemy units

### Scout X
Units this abilty may make a move up to X at the start of the game but must end their move move than 9 inches away from any enemy units. If both players have units with this ability, roll off. Alternate between moving scout units starting with the player who won the roll. 

### Blast
Weapons with this ability may not be fired within engagement range or into units that are within engagement range of one or more units from your army. When targeting non MONSTER/VEHICLE units, for every 5 models in the target unit that are visible to this unit, add 1 to the attacks charactersitic of this weapon. If the target unit has the MONSTER/VEHICLE keyword, add 3 to the attacks characteristic instead.
