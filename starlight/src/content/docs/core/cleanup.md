### Battleshock tests
If a unit lost one or more models, or has taken 3 or more wounds/hull points worth of damage this turn if it is a MONSTER or VEHICLE, its controlling player must roll 2d6. If the result is less than the units cool characteristic the test is failed and the unit becomes battleshocked. If the unit is below half strength(ie half its wounds/hull points or half of its starting models), subtract 1 from that test. 


### Battleshock
While a unit is battleshocked, it has an OC characteristic of 0 and cannot be targeted with strategems.
